Instructions

Game Outline

Can you draw a maze in 60 seconds that takes more than 30 seconds to solve?

Clicking the Start button begins Player 1's turn. Player 1 is presented with a blank board, containing two dots: their green dot and Player 2's red dot.

Player 1 has 60 seconds to draw a maze between themselves and Player 2. Click on blank sections of the board to draw walls. Click on walls to erase them.

When time is up, the board is hidden and no more walls can be drawn. Then Player 2 can click to begin their turn.

Player 2's turn begins and they have 30 seconds to draw a line from their dot to Player 1's dot. They must start with their own dot to draw their line.

The game ends when Player 2 reaches Player 1, or when Player 2's timer runs out.

1 or 2 Player Mode

On the Start page, click the Player Mode button to switch between 1 or 2 Player Mode.

In 1-Player mode, you will take the role of Player 1 and the computer will take the role of Player 2.

In 2-Player mode, make sure that Player 2 can't see the screen while Player 1 is drawing their map. You want to make sure they only have their 30 seconds to solve the map!

Tips!

  • Diagonal movement is allowed. A diagonal wall won't stop Player 2 from traveling along the perpendicular diagonal. Fill in those gaps to make it solid!

  • The computer is very smart. It (usually) won't follow a blind alley. The best strategy against the computer is to make your path as long and byzantine as possible!

  • Walling yourself off is not allowed. If Player 1 has walled themself off from Player 2 and there is no possible path between them, then Player 2 wins!
